Rippchen

Description

Delicious ribs from the grill. Always go! And if you want to keep your figure, you have to grill sometimes, even though you're not hungry.

Ingredients

  • Enough (!) ribs
  • Grill
  • Beer and Friends

Steps

  1. Open beer for every visitor
  2. Grill ribs until done
  3. Open another beer for every visitor
  4. Enjoy
